Gravity zipline
soar high above the treetops on a zipline trip that’s unlike anything you’ve ever experienced! Gravity Ziplines are for those looking for the thrill of speed and the rush of flying high above the treetops! On this gravity powered zipline, you won’t have to use your hands to brake on the cable. Instead take in the sights as you zoom high above the valley below. The Gravity Ziplines tour serves up a dose of adrenaline that’s on par with our most extreme adventures! You’ll zip up to 200 feet high and over one and a half miles worth of cables, all through a wonderful series of mountain ridges and bowls in the heart of Southern West Virginia. The course descends six ziplines: two 500-foot warm-ups, and then progressively longer and faster lines at 1,300 feet, 1,600 feet, and 1,800 feet before getting to the main attraction. All that adrenaline so far? That’s just a preview of the thrill you’ll experience on Adrena-line! This 3,150-foot zipline (that’s about three quarters of a mile) is one of the longest and fastest in the nation, pushing zippers up to 65 miles per hour. Give yourself to Gravity. It’ll be a highlight of your trip!

Lower new river whitewater rafting
On our Lower New Whitewater Rafting Trip, adventure seekers will raft through the heart of the New River Gorge National Park and Preserve. Here, you’ll travel on this ancient waterway, learning about the history of the region while experiencing incredible rapids, calm pools perfect for swimming, and Jump Rock. As the rapids get bigger, so do the boulders strewn along the sides of the river. It’s truly a sight you won’t want to miss, and since it’s a full day trip, you don’t have to! Spending a full day on the Lower New River and experiencing rapids, scenery, and creating memories to last a lifetime, is an adventure you will be glad you made. Don’t think that a full day experience whitewater rafting the Lower New River Gorge is just for professionals. On the contrary! This Lower New River Whitewater Rafting trip is a favorite for first timers and enthusiasts alike!
